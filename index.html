About Us

SAMADIA QURANIA MADRASA is an international relief and development public charitable trust established in 2022. Our institution is dedicated to providing quality education while preserving Islamic values and heritage.

History of the Madrasah

SAMADIA QURANIA MADRASA started its journey in 2022. The local people, mostly from poor families in Kakdwip, South 24 Parganas, established this institution to provide education to their children while maintaining their cultural and religious identity.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

Our vision is to create a conducive learning environment that nurtures the growth of well-rounded individuals imbued with the values of humanity, responsibility, and global citizenship.

Mission

Our mission is to impart quality education and instill a spirit of excellence, enabling students to embrace challenges, uphold cultural heritage, and develop a sense of true nationalism and patriotism.

Academic

SAMADIA QURANIA MADRASA offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes:

  • Arabic, Urdu, English, and Bengali as mediums of instruction
  • Subjects like Al Aqaid (Belief in Islam), Fiqh (Jurisprudence), Tazkiya (Moral Lessons), and Quran Studies
  • Vocational training and computer education
  • Modern education alongside traditional Islamic teachings
