About Us

SAMADIA QURANIA MADARASA is an international relief and development public charitable Trust established in 2022. Inspired by the Islamic teachings of empathy, generosity, and selflessness, the trust aims to alleviate poverty and suffering across the world. The Messenger of Allah Sallallahu ‘alaihiwasallam said, ‘The believers, in their love, mutual kindness, and close ties, are like one body; when any part complains, the whole body responds to it with wakefulness and fever.’

We provide sustainable development solutions relating to shelter, education, health, and spiritual well-being to those who are neglected and oppressed.

History of the Madrasah

SAMADIA QURANIA MADARASA started its journey in 2022. The local people, mostly from poor families in Kakdwip, South 24 Parganas, established this institution to provide education to their children. Initially, the Madrasah operated in mud-walled or thatched classrooms with a few students. Over time, the number of students grew, and today, it exceeds 100. This growth is a testament to the hard work of the teachers and guardians.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

Our vision is to create a conducive learning environment that nurtures the growth of well-rounded individuals imbued with the values of humanity, responsibility, and global citizenship.

Mission

Our mission is to impart quality education and instill a spirit of excellence, enabling students to embrace challenges, uphold cultural heritage, and develop a sense of true nationalism and patriotism.

Academic

SAMADIA QURANIA MADARASA offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes:

  • Arabic, Urdu, English, and Bengali as mediums of instruction.
  • Subjects like Al Aqaid (Belief in Islam), Fiqh (Jurisprudence), Tazkiya (Moral Lessons), and Quran Studies.
  • Vocational training and computer education.
